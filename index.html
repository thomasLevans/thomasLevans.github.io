My passion is the visualization of data. This goes beyond the creation of a visually appealing representation. Rather it is the art of gently exposing the various meanings hidden in data. When done well, beauty follows. My brush is D3.js, my canvas Javascript, and the colors unfold from whatever data I can get my hands on. I am in the early formative years of my career, and my passion to develop my skills has never been stronger. Great under pressure, my methods are adaptable and fluid.

Poke around my projects, some are still under development while others are merely proofs of concept. My current focus is the development of a translational science dashboard for the Medical University of South Carolina. Check out my Github, LinkedIn, and my resume.

Translational Science Dashboard

Developed for the Medical University of South Carolina with the cooperation of the Biometrics Informatics Research Group, the dashboard has been an ongoing project of mine since Janruary 2015. Leveraging MUSC's participation in Open Linked Data, the dashboard allows the user to track the research trends of an institution in terms of the bridge from basic to clinical science based research.

Checkout the live demo!

Topsoil

Developed in the Cyber Infrastructure Research and Development Lab for the Earth Sciences (CIRDLES), Topsoil is a visualization tool build on the Java 8 webkit to geochronologists in sample data visualization. Topsoil's visualizations are powered by the D3.js library, leveraging easily exportable scalable vector graphics. Over a period of five months I aided in the refinement of Topsoil's chart rendering, and integrated unit testing via Karma.js.

Checkout the Project Site!

MUSC Clinical Trial Word Cloud

This project never moved beyond proof of concept. The purpose was to see what data regarding clinical trials associated with the Medical University of South Carolina was available via the LinkedCT.gov SPARQL endpoint.

Checkout the live demo!

Fullstack Database Project w/ AngularJS Frontend

The original project was to design and build a database backend for a mock business. The middle and frontend were merely to serve to demonstrate the functionality of the underlying database. I took the opportunity to build a complete fully functional, full stack web app. The frontend utilizes Angular.js and D3.js, the middle layer is built on PHP, with a MySQL database on the backend.

Checkout the live demo!
